About Us

Cars that shines like diamonds

Our History

Founded in 1998, DiamondCut Motors has grown exponentially to become a prestigious name in the luxury car market in Indonesia. Our journey began with a simple goal: to provide the finest automobiles to discerning customers. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others.
From the very beginning, DiamondCut Motors has been dedicated to offering cutting-edge service, timely delivery, and a steadfast commitment to vehicle safety. Our reputation for excellence has attracted a loyal clientele of business owners and successful individuals who seek the best in luxury vehicles. As we continue to grow, we remain committed to our core values of quality, customer satisfaction, innovation, and integrity. DiamondCut Motors is proud to be a leader in the luxury automotive market, and we look forward to serving our customers with the same dedication and passion that has defined our success since 1998.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of luxury vehicles in Indonesia, setting the standard for excellence in customer service, quality, and innovation in the automotive industry.

Our Mission

  • Deliver Excellence: To offer the finest selection of luxury vehicles, ensuring the highest standards of quality and performance.
  • Customer Satisfaction: To prioritize the needs and preferences of our customers, providing personalized service and an exceptional buying experience.
  • Innovation: To continuously seek innovative solutions and adopt cutting-edge technologies to enhance our services and products.
  • Integrity: To operate with the highest level of integrity, building trust and long-lasting relationships with our customers, partners, and the community.
